Gentrification

Gentrification is a site built to encourage community dialogue around the sensitve subject, with the hope of finding solutions that positively integrate gentrifying communities. We support open and honest communication from all aspects, both good and bad, while protecting our participants and viewers by implementing guidelines that keep our forum safe and respectful. We do not allow nor condone any language that is degrading or disrespectful to any group of people and/or individual. Our hope is to have safe, thriving, and diverse communities across the nation. Starting with the important conversation concerning Gentrification.
